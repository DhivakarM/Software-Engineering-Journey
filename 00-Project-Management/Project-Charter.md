This project represents my transition from Technical Project Management to hands-on Software Engineering.

After leading software teams for more than two decades, I want to deepen my practical engineering skills by building real-world applications, documenting my learning journey, and sharing my knowledge through open-source repositories.

## Business Need

After 22 years of experience as a Technical Project Manager and 2 years as a Business Partner at HDFC Life, I want to strengthen my software engineering skills and apply them to solving real-world financial planning problems.

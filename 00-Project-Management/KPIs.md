# Purpose

This document defines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used to measure the progress, quality, consistency, and success of the Software Engineering Journey project.

Unlike OKRs, which define strategic objectives, KPIs measure how effectively those objectives are being achieved throughout the project.

---

# KPI Categories

## 1. Learning Progress

| KPI | Target | Review Frequency |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Learning Sessions Completed | Minimum 2 per week | Weekly |
| Planned Modules Completed | 100% | Monthly |
| Practical Exercises Completed | 100% | Monthly |

---

## 2. Project Execution

| KPI | Target | Review Frequency |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Sprint Completion Rate | 100% | End of Sprint |
| Planned Deliverables Completed | ≥90% | Monthly |
| Milestones Achieved On Time | ≥90% | Monthly |

---

## 3. Documentation Quality

| KPI | Target | Review Frequency |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Documents Reviewed Before Approval | 100% | Weekly |
| Documentation Updated | After every major milestone | Weekly |
| Revision History Maintained | 100% | Monthly |

---

## 4. Git & GitHub Activity

| KPI | Target | Review Frequency |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Meaningful Git Commits | Every completed session | Weekly |
| GitHub Push Frequency | Every completed session | Weekly |
| Repository Status | No pending work after session | Weekly |

---

## 5. FinAdvisor-Pro Development

| KPI | Target | Review Frequency |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Features Completed | As per Sprint Plan | Monthly |
| Prototype Releases | As planned | Quarterly |
| Architecture Documents Completed | 100% | Monthly |

---

## 6. Continuous Improvement

| KPI | Target | Review Frequency |
|------|--------|------------------|
| Lessons Learned Updated | Every Sprint | Sprint Review |
| Risks Reviewed | Monthly | Monthly |
| Improvement Actions Implemented | At least 1 per Sprint | Sprint Review |

---

# KPI Review Process

- Weekly Review
- Sprint Review
- Monthly Review
- Quarterly Review

---

# Success Criteria

The project is considered healthy when:

- Learning schedule is consistently maintained.
- Sprint goals are achieved.
- Documentation remains current.
- GitHub repositories are actively maintained.
- FinAdvisor-Pro progresses according to the roadmap.
